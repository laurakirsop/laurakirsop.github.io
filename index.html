About

The mythology (also called mytharc) refers to a series of episodes in The X-Files that primarily focuses on an overarching story, involving the efforts of Mulder and Scully to investigate and expose the conspiracy to suppress knowledge of the existence of extraterrestrials - read more.

We are watching all the mytharc episodes from the beginning and I am writing notes to help me remember it all.
